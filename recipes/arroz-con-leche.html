Arroz Con Leche

Delicious arroz con leche

this dish can be served both hot or cold


Ingredients

  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 cup uncooked white rice
  • 2 1/2 cups of milk
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar, or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cinnamon stick

Steps

  1. combine water and rice in a bowl, let soak at least 30 minutes
  2. bring water and rice to boil, reduce to medium-low heat and cover
  3. simmer until rice is tender and liquid has been absorbed (20-25 minutes)
  4. stir milk, sugar, salt and cinnamon. simmer until desire thickness
  5. discard cinnamon stick before serving
